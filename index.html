DEPTHS OF WONDER

HOW TO PLAY

  • Use the Up Arrow Key to make the underwater diver swim upwards, avoiding obstacles in the way.
  • Use the Left Arrow Key to move the diver to the left and the Right Arrow Key to move the diver to the right, navigating through the underwater challenges.
  • Use the Down Arrow key to move the diver down.
  • Be careful not to touch any of the obstacles, as it will result in a game over.
  • Gather 10 Points to win the round.
